About The Fest

Where Innovation Meets Celebration

The Department of Computer Applications proudly presents Tech Fêté 2K26, a grand technical and cultural extravaganza happening at Pope John Paul II College of Education, Puducherry.

Tech Fêté 2K26 is designed to bring together creativity, innovation, logic, and talent under one platform. This event aims to inspire students to showcase their technical expertise, problem-solving skills, artistic creativity, and teamwork through a series of exciting competitions and activities.

Whether you are a tech enthusiast, a creative thinker, or a performer at heart, Tech Fêté offers something for everyone. Join us for a day filled with learning, competition, collaboration, and unforgettable memories.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before registering.

Who can participate in Tech Fêté 2K26?
Students from any recognized college or university pursuing UG/PG courses can participate. Please ensure you bring a valid college ID card on the day of the event, as it is mandatory for entry.
Can I register for multiple events?
Absolutely! You can add multiple events to your selection cart before checking out. However, please make sure the timings of the events do not clash. Our system will automatically alert you if you select events happening at the exact same time.
Are on-spot registrations available?
Yes, on-spot registrations will be open from 8:30 AM to 9:30 AM at the reception desk on the day of the event. However, this is strictly subject to slot availability. We highly recommend registering online in advance to secure your spot.
Will food and refreshments be provided?
Lunch will be provided complimentary for participants who register for 2 or more events. For others, a canteen is available on campus. Basic refreshments and water will be available for all attendees throughout the day.
