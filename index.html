About The Fest

Where Innovation Meets Celebration

The Department of Computer Applications proudly organises Tech Fête 2K26, a grand technical and cultural extravaganza happening at Pope John Paul II College of Education, Puducherry.

Tech Fête 2K26 is designed to bring together creativity, innovation, logic, and talent under one platform. This event aims to inspire students to showcase their technical expertise, problem-solving skills, artistic creativity, and teamwork through a series of exciting competitions and activities.

Whether you are a tech enthusiast, a creative thinker, or a performer at heart, Tech Fête offers something for everyone. Join us for a day filled with learning, competition, collaboration, and unforgettable memories.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before registering.

Who can participate in Tech Fête 2K26?
Students from any recognized college or university pursuing UG/PG courses can participate. Please ensure you bring a valid college ID card on the day of the event, as it is mandatory for entry.
Can I register for multiple events?
Absolutely! You can participate in any number of individual events, and up to a maximum of 2 group events (maximum 3 events total per checkout). However, please make sure the timings of the events do not clash.
Will food and refreshments be provided?
Lunch and refreshments will be provided to anyone who is registering for the events.
